New Yorkers care about their restaurant quality, especially Staten Island

Since 2015, Staten Island has seen the highest percentage of A-rated restaurants

By Adrian O'Farrill
November 22, 2024

Staten Island has a reputation for being the outcast out of the five New York City boroughs. But if you decide to make the trek to this misunderstood island, you have a high chance of dining at an A-rated restaurant.

According to data from NYC's Department of Health, 83% of Staten Island's restaurants have had an A-rating since 2015, the most of all the five boroughs. While Manhattan might have had nearly ten times as many restaurants in that time span, its A-rated restaurants made up 80%.
